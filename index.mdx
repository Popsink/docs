**Move, model and use your data in real time.**

Automatically capture, transform, and deliver your data at sub-second speeds to the products, systems, and people that depend on it. Overcome the costs & limitations of conventional ELT tools and set a new standard for your operations and analytics.

## What is Popsink?

Popsink is a powerful data integration platform that enables real-time data capture, transformation, and delivery. Our platform helps you:

- **Capture data changes** in real-time from various sources
- **Transform data** on-the-fly with powerful processing capabilities
- **Deliver data** to multiple destinations with sub-second latency
- **Scale effortlessly** from small datasets to enterprise-level data volumes
