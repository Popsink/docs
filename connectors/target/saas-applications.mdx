This family groups the destinations designed to reinject Popsink data into your business tools or internal applications.

> For shared authentication, governance, and monitoring parameters, refer to the [target connectors overview](./overview).

## Custom applications

- [Generic Application](./application) – Integrate Popsink with your applications through our REST API.

## Collaborative & CRM SaaS

- [Slack](./slack) – Send contextual notifications to your Slack channels.
- [HubSpot](./hubspot) – Enrich HubSpot CRM objects with consolidated data.
- [Airtable](./airtable) – Populate Airtable bases for your operational workflows.

Need to connect another application? [support@popsink.com](mailto:support@popsink.com) can help build a dedicated connector.
