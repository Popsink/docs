Popsink can run within your data center or private cloud environment, giving you complete control over your data infrastructure while maintaining all the powerful capabilities of the Popsink platform.

## Overview

The On-Premises deployment model is ideal for organizations with:
- Strict data residency requirements
- Existing data center infrastructure
- Compliance mandates requiring on-prem solutions
- Air-gapped or isolated network environments

## System Requirements

### Hardware

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Minimum Requirements" icon="server">
    - **CPU:** 8 cores
    - **RAM:** 32 GB
    - **Storage:** 500 GB SSD
    - **Network:** 1 Gbps
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Recommended (Production)" icon="building">
    - **CPU:** 16+ cores
    - **RAM:** 64+ GB
    - **Storage:** 1+ TB NVMe SSD
    - **Network:** 10 Gbps
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Software

- **Container Runtime:** Docker 20.10+ or containerd 1.6+
- **Orchestration:** Kubernetes 1.24+ (recommended) or Docker Compose
- **Operating System:** Ubuntu 20.04+, RHEL 8+, or equivalent
- **Database:** PostgreSQL 14+ (for Popsink metadata)

## Responsibilities

### Customer
- Provide physical/virtual infrastructure
- Manage storage and networking
- Configure identity and access management
- Monitor system health
- Perform backups and disaster recovery

### Popsink
- Provide software and configuration
- Deliver deployment instructions
- Support integration with observability systems
- Provide guidance on upgrades and troubleshooting
